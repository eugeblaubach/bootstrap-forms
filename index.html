Suprematism Quiz

Suprematism, not to be confused with supremacism, is an avant-garde art movement that emerged in Russia around 1913. The movement is characterized by a robust use of geometric shapes and a limited color palette.

Read this exerpt from The Non-Objective World (1927).

Under Suprematism I understand the primacy of pure feeling in creative art. To the Suprematist, the visual phenomena of the objective world are, in themselves, meaningless; the significant thing is feeling, as such, quite apart from the environment in which it is called forth.
